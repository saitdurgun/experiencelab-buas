# Earlier Projects

Here is an overview of selected projects carried out in and by the Experience Lab.

---

## 1. Van Gogh Storysperience
Studying the relationship between storytelling, experience, and memory using EEG measurements in the lab.

## 2. Experiencing a VR Roller Coaster
Measuring skin conductance during a roller coaster ride at Europapark Germany, comparing experiences with and without a VR add-on.

## 3. JOIN: Youth in an Inclusive Society
Measuring emotional engagement of young adults with varying backgrounds during cultural experiences.

## 4. PhD: Measuring Emotions and Experiences
PhD research by Wim Strijbosch on how temporal dynamics of emotions relate to how experiences are remembered and evaluated.

## 5. Self-Driving Transportation in Drimmelen
Studying passenger comfort and emotional responses during rides in a self-driving bus.

## 6. Experiencing Autonomic Transportation
Mapping emotional experiences over a journey route to identify moments of comfort and discomfort in autonomous vehicles.
