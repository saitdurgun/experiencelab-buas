# Earlier Projects
Here is an overview of selected projects carried out in and by the Experience Lab.

---

## 1. Van Gogh Storysperience
This research project helps Van Gogh Brabant in the redesign of three visitor centers across the province by providing insights into potential visitors' experiences with narrative presentation.

[View project on BUas website](https://www.buas.nl/en/research/professorships/storytelling/project-vangogh-storysperience){: .btn .btn-outline }

---

## 2. Experiencing a VR Roller Coaster
Measuring skin conductance during a roller coaster ride at Europapark Germany, comparing experiences with and without a VR add-on.

[View project on BUas website](https://www.buas.nl/en/research/professorships/leisure-and-tourism-experiences/project-experiencing-vr-roller-coaster){: .btn .btn-outline }

---

## 3. JOIN: Youth in an Inclusive Society
Measuring emotional engagement of young adults with varying backgrounds during cultural experiences.

[View project on BUas website](https://www.buas.nl/en/research/professorships/leisure-and-tourism-experiences/project-measuring-emotions-and-experiences){: .btn .btn-outline }

---

## 4. PhD: Measuring Emotions and Experiences
PhD research by Wim Strijbosch on how temporal dynamics of emotions relate to how experiences are remembered and evaluated.

[View project on BUas website](https://www.buas.nl/en/research/professorships/leisure-and-tourism-experiences/project-measuring-emotions-and-experiences){: .btn .btn-outline }

---

## 5. Self-Driving Transportation in Drimmelen
Studying passenger comfort and emotional responses during rides in a self-driving bus.

[View project on BUas website](https://www.buas.nl/en/research/professorships/smart-cities-and-logistics/project-self-driving-transportation-in-drimmelen){: .btn .btn-outline }

---

## 6. Experiencing Autonomic Transportation — LEVERAGE
The LEVERAGE consortium aims to explore and enhance the potential of Light Electric Vehicles (LEVs), such as e-scooters, e-steps, e-bikes, and micro-cars, for the mobility transition.

[View project on BUas website](https://www.buas.nl/en/research/professorships/urban-mobility-planning/leverage){: .btn .btn-outline }
